## Overview
This dashboard translates real nonprofit program and funding data into clear performance indicators that support leadership, funders, and community stakeholders.

> Dashboards are built using real organizational data. Public materials are anonymized to protect privacy and funder confidentiality.

## Business Questions
- Are funded programs achieving intended outcomes?
- How do outcomes vary across impact areas and geographies?
- Where do investments generate the strongest results?

## Core Metrics
- Total individuals served
- Outcome success rates
- Investment by impact area
- Geographic reach of services

## Key Insights
- Programs across impact areas consistently demonstrated high outcome success rates
- Health-focused initiatives showed particularly strong performance relative to investment
- Geographic patterns revealed opportunities for targeted service expansion

## Business Impact
Supports grant reporting, board oversight, and outcome-driven funding decisions.

## Summary (Organizations are anonymized with Real data displayed)
- Across all impact areas, programs demonstrated strong outcome success rates, with multiple initiatives achieving 90%+ effectiveness, indicating well-aligned service design and delivery.
- Healthy Community investments showed particularly high returns, with trauma-informed training and healthcare access producing consistent, measurable outcomes.
- Geographic analysis revealed concentrated service demand in specific counties, highlighting opportunities for targeted expansion and resource alignment.
- Outcome performance varied more by program design and service type than by total dollars invested, reinforcing the importance of outcome-focused funding decisions.
These Key Findings directly support:
- Funder and grant compliance reporting
- Board-level strategic planning
- Resource allocation and program scaling decisions
